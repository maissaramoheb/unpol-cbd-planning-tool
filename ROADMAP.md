# ROADMAP

This roadmap outlines the planned development path for the **UNPOL Capacity-Building & Development (CBD) Integrated Planning Tool**. It highlights both long-term goals and near-term maintainer priorities to enhance the utility, security, and onboarding experience for police advisors and peace operations planners.

---

## 🛠️ Near-Term Maintainer Roadmap

These items are targeted for upcoming releases to improve usability and maintainer evidence:

- **Country-Based PESTEL-S Starter Templates**: Preload generalized baseline templates representing standard operational environments (e.g., post-conflict transition, institutional reform, high-security threats) to accelerate initial diagnosis.
- **Improved Export Formatting**: Refine the cover-sheet PDF preview print layouts and Markdown outputs to align with standard UN briefing and report templates.
- **Sample Mission Scenarios**: Provide educational walkthrough data files demonstrating a completed analysis cycle for fictional, doctrine-aligned peacekeeping and advisory missions.
- **Responsible AI-Assisted Review Workflow**: Integrate an optional, private, and fully local web-based LLM assist (using WebGPU or lightweight local models) to suggest capacity indicators and prioritize sequencing without transmitting planning data.
- **Security & Privacy Hardening**: Implement IndexedDB storage migration to enable encrypted workspace options and support larger multi-profile portfolios without performance degradation.
- **Better Documentation & Onboarding**: Develop an interactive walkthrough tutorial for new users, detailing UNPOL capacity-building terminology and standard PESTEL-S category definitions.

---

## 🗺️ Long-Term Vision

Our long-term goals focus on improving reference-aligned planning support using publicly available guidance, while preserving the project’s unofficial educational status.

### 1. Reference-Aligned Doctrine Support
- Preload standards and guidelines from DPKO/DFS, United Nations Police Capacity-Building and Development manuals, and human rights due diligence policy.
- Link monitoring and evaluation indicators directly to standard UN police performance frameworks.

### 2. Multi-Profile Portfolio Management
- Allow planning officers to manage, compare, and merge multiple regional or provincial police profiles within the same dashboard workspace.

### 3. Federated Off-Grid Collaboration
- Research peer-to-peer secure offline syncing protocols to allow advisory teams in low-connectivity mission environments to collaborate on plans without internet dependency.
